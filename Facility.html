Facility

Swimming Pool

Indoor swimming pool is a get away place you have to vist if you are staying at Grand Oceania Harbourfont as it is 500m wide and 400m long. It is a good get away place from crowded areas, event of rain or simply craving for some indoor relaxation.
Outdoor swimming pool is 700m wide and 600m long with an open view facing VivoCity and there is a playground for your kids to play also!

Operation Hour: 10am to 10pm
Location: Level 2 (Indoor) and Level 6 (Outdoor)

    Things to Note:
  1. First Come First Server basis
  2. Book a slot before visiting the area
  3. Each slot has a time limit of 1 hour with a limit of 10 pax in the area

We apologise for the inconvience caused. Book your slot for the Swimming Pool here!
