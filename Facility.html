Facility

Pool


Indoor :

Indoor swimming pool is a get away place you have to vist if you are staying at Grand Oceania Harbourfont as 500m wide and 400m long, it's a good place to get away from crowded areas such as the outdoor swimming pool or in the event of rain. It is located at level 2.


Outdoor :

Outdoor swimming pool is 700m wide and 600m long and it has an open view to Vivo City and Sentosa located at the rooftop level 6.

Operation Hour: 10am to 10pm
Things to Note:

  1. First Come First Server basis
  2. Book a slot before visiting the area
  3. Each slot has a time limit of 1 hour with a limit of 10 people in the area
